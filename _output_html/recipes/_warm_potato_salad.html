Warm Potato Salad

15 min
15 min
8 Servings

Ingredients

Preparation

  • Chop the chives and dill, mince the onion. Halve the potatoes and eggs, and dice the bacon.

Cooking

  1. Heat a skillet over medium high heat. Add bacon and cook until brown and crispy. Drain excess fat, transfer bacon to a paper towel-lined plate.
  2. Place potatoes in a medium saucepan and cover with cold salted water by 1 inch. Bring to a boil and simmer until just tender.
  3. In a large bowl, whisk together olive oil, Dijon, red wine vinegar, shallot, chives and dill, season with salt and pepper.
  4. Stir in potatoes and bacon until well combined.
  5. Serve immediately, topped with eggs.
