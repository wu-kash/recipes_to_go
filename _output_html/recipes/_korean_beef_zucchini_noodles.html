Korean Beef Zucchini Noodles

15 min
10 min
4 Servings

Ingredients

Preparation

  • Grate the ginger, mince the garlic and thinly slice the green onions.

Cooking

  1. In a small bowl, whisk together brown sugar, soy sauce, ginger, sesame oil and Sriracha.
  2. Heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium high heat. Add garlic and cook, stirring constantly, until fragrant, about 1 minute. Add ground beef and cook until browned, making sure to crumble the beef as it cooks. Salt to taste.
  3. Stir in zucchini noodles, green onions and soy sauce mixture until well combined, allowing to simmer until heated through, about 2 minutes.
  4. Garnish with sesame seeds and serve.
