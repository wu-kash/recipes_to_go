Spicy Tuna Avocado Wrap

10 min
4 Servings

Ingredients

Preparation

  • Chop the carrots into matchsticks and the rest of the ingredients.

Cooking

  1. In a medium bowl, mash together tuna and avocado until combined. Add in the rest of the ingredients through the salt and pepper, mixing well.
  2. To assemble, top each tortilla with a sfrac{1}{2} cup leafy greens, sfrac{1}{4} cup matchstick carrots and divide the tuna mixture evenly among the wraps.
  3. Tightly roll up the tortilla, slice and enjoy!
