Chicken Fajitas

15 min
15 min
4 Servings

Ingredients

Preparation

  • Cut the chicken breasts into thin strips. Cut the bell peppers and onion into slices. Mince the garlic and chop the cilantro leaves.

Cooking

  1. In a small bowl, combine chili powder, cumin, oregano, paprika, and add salt and pepper, to taste. Toss the chicken strips with the mixture.
  2. Add the olive oil over medium heat into a pan. Add the garlic and chicken strips, and cook until just cooked.
  3. Add the onions and sliced peppers and cook for an additional 2 minutes.
  4. Serve with tortillas and squeeze lime juice over the mix.
