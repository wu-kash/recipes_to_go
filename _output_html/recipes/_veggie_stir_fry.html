Veggie Stir Fry

10 min
10 min
4 Servings

Ingredients

Preparation

  • Prepare the vegetables by cleaning and chopping the vegetables into smaller pieces.

Cooking

  1. Put the oil into a pan on moderate heat and crush the garlic into the pan. Stir it around.
  2. Add the spring onion, sweet pepper and mange tout. Fry and stir for about 30 seconds.
  3. Add the bean sprouts and soy sauce. Stir fry for about 2 minutes.
  4. Serve with cooked noodles.
