Spaghetti Carbonara

5 min
15 min
4 Servings

Ingredients

Preparation

  • Grate the Parmessan cheese, dice the bacon, chop the parsley and mince the garlic.

Cooking

  1. In a large pot of boiling salted water, cook pasta according to package instructions, reserve sfrac{1}{2} cup water and drain well.
  2. In a small bowl, whisk together eggs and Parmesan and set aside.
  3. Heat a large cast iron skillet over medium high heat. Add bacon and cook until brown and crispy, about 6-8 minutes and reserve excess fat.
  4. Stir in garlic until fragrant, about 1 minute. Reduce heat to low.
  5. Working quickly, stir in pasta and egg mixture, and gently toss to combine, season with salt and pepper, to taste. Add reserved pasta water, one tablespoon at a time, until desired consistency is reached.
  6. Garnish with parsley.
