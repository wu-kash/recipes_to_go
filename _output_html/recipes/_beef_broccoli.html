Beef Broccoli

5 min
10 min
4 Servings

Ingredients

Preparation

  • Thinly slice the flank steak and cut the broccoli head into florets. Mince the garlic.

Cooking

  1. In a medium bowl, whisk together soy sauce, chicken stock, honey, vinegar, brown sugar, garlic, sesame oil, cornstarch, Sriracha, ginger, red pepper flakes and sfrac{1}{4} cup water; set aside.
  2. Heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium high heat. Add steak and cook, flipping once, until browned, about 3-4 minutes.
  3. Stir in broccoli and soy sauce mixture until tender and slightly thickened, about 3-4 minutes.
