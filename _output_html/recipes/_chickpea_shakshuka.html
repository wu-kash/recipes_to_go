Chickpea Shakshuka

5 min
25 min
6 Servings

Ingredients

Preparation

  • Dice the onion, chop the red bell pepper and mince the garlic. Halve the olives.

Cooking

  1. Heat a large rimmed metal or cast iron skillet over medium heat. Once hot, add olive oil, onion, bell pepper and garlic. Saut√© for 4-5 minutes, stirring frequently until soft.
  2. Add crushed tomatoes, tomato paste, coconut sugar, sea salt, paprika, cumin, chili powder, cinnamon, cayenne pepper, cardamom, and coriander.
  3. Bring to a simmer over medium heat and cook for 2-3 minutes, stirring frequently.
  4. Add chickpeas and olives, reduce heat to medium-low and simmer for 15-20 minutes. Taste and adjust seasonings as needed.
  5. Serve as is or with bread, pasta, or rice. Garnish with fresh lemon juice, additional olives, and cilantro or parsley for extra flavor.
