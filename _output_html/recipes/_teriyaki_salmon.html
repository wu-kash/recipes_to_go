Teriyaki Salmon

10 min
20 min
4 Servings

Ingredients

Preparation

  • Thinly slice the green onion, peel and cut the carrots into slices.

Cooking

  1. In a large saucepan of 2 cups water, cook rice according to package instructions and set aside.
  2. Preheat oven to tempFToC{400}. Lightly oil a baking sheet or coat with nonstick spray.
  3. Place salmon, green beans and carrots in a single layer onto the prepared baking sheet.
  4. Spoon teriyaki sauce over the salmon.
  5. Drizzle green beans and carrots with olive oil, season with salt and pepper to taste.
  6. Place into oven and cook until the fish flakes easily with a fork, about 15 minutes.
  7. Serve with rice, garnished with green onions and sesame seeds.
