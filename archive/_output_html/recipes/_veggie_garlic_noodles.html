Veggie Garlic Noodles

5 min
10 min
4 Servings

Ingredients

Preparation

  • Cut the carrots into matchsticks, slice the green onion and mince the garlic.

Cooking

  1. Cook the rice noodles according to he instructions.
  2. Heat the vegetable oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Add the garlic and 3 green onions and cook for 2-3 minutes, until fragrant and tender. Add the carrots and snap peas, then stir in the brown sugar and soy sauce.
  3. Add the noodles and toss for a couple of minutes, until the noodles are well-coated and the vegetables are tender. Season with salt and pepper.
  4. Serve immediately, garnished with the remaining green onion.
